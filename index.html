About Me

There hasn't been a time in life where I wasn't drawing. Ever since I could hold a pencil, I've been obsessed with whatever stories I could imagine. I love drawing on all of my other interests and combing them into something (or someone) new. A lot of what I do is driven by character development and interaction that is only possible amongst huan beings. Though I am pretty shy, here is something about the human experience that is both terrifying and overwhelmingly fascinating.
Outside of art I'm an 80s music aficianado, history buff, anime and video game nerd, and an overall child at heart.
